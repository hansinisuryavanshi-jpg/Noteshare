Frequently Asked Questions
What AI tools are included in Pro?+
Pro includes three AI tools: Flashcard Generator (creates Q&A cards from your notes), Short Notes / TL;DR (condenses long notes into key bullet points), and Mind Map Builder (visual concept maps). All powered by Claude AI.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
Is there a student discount?+
Yes! Verify your college email (.edu or institution email) and get an additional 20% off any plan. The yearly Pro plan already comes at ₹59/month — verify your student status to bring it to ₹47/month.
How many AI generations do I get?+
Pro plan includes 50 AI generations per month. Each flashcard deck, short notes summary, or mind map counts as one generation. Unused generations don't carry over. Campus Pack users get unlimited generations.
